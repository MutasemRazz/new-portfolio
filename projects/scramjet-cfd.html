🔥 Scramjet CFD Analysis

Course: Aircraft & Rocket Propulsion
Role: CFD Analyst
Tools: OpenFOAM, ParaView, MATLAB, VisIt

✅ Objectives

  • Perform CFD simulation of compressible flow through a scramjet combustion chamber using OpenFOAM.
  • Analyze shock wave interactions and combustion zone behavior at supersonic speeds.

📊 Key Results

  • Simulation captured shock trains and shock-boundary layer interaction in supersonic regime.
  • Observed combustion zone stabilization and thermal boundary layer development.
  • Used residual convergence analysis to validate steady-state conditions.

🛠️ Your Contributions

  • Built custom 2D geometry and mesh using blockMesh for scramjet inlet and combustor section.
  • Implemented turbulence and compressibility models using rhoCentralFoam solver.
  • Post-processed results using ParaView to visualize shock structures and Mach contours.
  • Prepared simulation report comparing different shock structures under varied inflow Mach numbers.

📦 Deliverables

  • Fully meshed scramjet geometry with CFD setup and boundary conditions.
  • Mach number contour plots and pressure distribution results.
  • Simulation report including analysis of boundary layers, stagnation pressure loss, and shock positioning.

🎥 Media (Recommended)

  • Contour visualizations of Mach number and pressure using ParaView
  • Comparison of inlet vs exit profiles under different inflow speeds
  • Animated shock propagation across combustion section
