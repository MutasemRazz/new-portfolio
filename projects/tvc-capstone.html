🚀 Thrust Vector Control (TVC) – Capstone Project

Course: Capstone Design Project
Role: Structures Lead
Tools: SolidWorks, ANSYS, Granta EduPack
Award: 🥇 First Place – Engineering Design Fair (Multidisciplinary Engineering Category)
Team Affiliation: SOAR Student Rocketry Team Foundation

✅ Objectives

  • Develop a cost-effective Thrust Vector Control (TVC) system using a jet vane mechanism for solid rocket motors.
  • Design and fabricate a vertical test stand with integrated instrumentation for ground validation.
  • Execute hot fire tests to validate the mechanical and software integration of the TVC system.
  • Provide the SOAR rocketry team with a proven baseline system for future flight implementation.

📊 Key Results

  • 4 successful hot fire tests conducted with full TVC actuation and real-time control.
  • Jet vane TVC system integrated with load cells, servos, microcontroller-based PID loop, and thrust measurement system.
  • Vertical test stand passed FEA safety checks under expected thrust loads (~200 N).
  • T-slot aluminum framing selected for modularity and precision, improving adaptability for future tests.
  • Real-time data acquired from load cells confirmed system responsiveness and control accuracy.

🛠️ Your Contributions

  • Engineering Design: Designed full 3D CAD models for vertical and horizontal test stands. Conducted FEA on structural components and motor mounts using ANSYS. Performed material selection using Granta EduPack and implemented design-for-safety principles.
  • Project Management: Managed procurement and fabrication of aluminum T-slot structures and load cell plates. Led vendor communication, ensuring cost-effective sourcing of materials. Scheduled practice presentations and coordinated with graduate labs for technical feedback.
  • Communication: Presented concepts and test plans to graduate students and course advisors. Drafted technical documentation and contributed to final report and presentations. Ensured team alignment and task delegation throughout design, assembly, and testing phases.

📦 Deliverables

  • Fully functional vertical test stand with TVC motor mount and integrated instrumentation.
  • Executable test plan with calibration, thrust measurements, and validation criteria.
  • Complete engineering report, including CAD files, FEA results, BOM, and videos.
  • TVC system baseline delivered to SOAR rocketry team for future development.
