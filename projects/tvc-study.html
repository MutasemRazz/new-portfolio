üìä Key Results

  • Fins removed ‚Üí Mass reduction of ~2 kg and lower drag ‚Üí higher apogee.
  • TVC offers control authority at low speeds and high AOA, unlike fins.
  • Stability:
    • Finned rocket: statically stable (1.6 caliber margin)
    • Finless rocket: unstable unless TVC is active
  • Jet vanes and jetavators found to be most practical for solid rockets with limited nozzle access.
  • Preferred Actuation: Servo-based Electromechanical Actuator (EMA) for precision and low weight.

  • Control integration with IMU and microcontroller for redundancy and safety.
